Built for real runs

Undead Intel is a zombie guide site focused on cleaner Easter Egg tracking, faster map navigation, and walkthroughs that are easier to use while you play.

What the site focuses on

  • Map-by-map Easter Egg guides.
  • Cleaner step tracking during gameplay.
  • Saved progress on your device.
  • Quick support for corrections, requests, and feature ideas.

Why it exists

Undead Intel is built to make guides faster to read, easier to use, and less frustrating to follow when you are actually in a run instead of reading a wall of text.
