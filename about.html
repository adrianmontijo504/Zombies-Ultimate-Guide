What Undead Intel is

Undead Intel is a zombies guide site built for fast map lookups, saved Easter Egg checklists, and cleaner walkthroughs that are easier to use during a live run.

What the site focuses on

  • Map-by-map Easter Egg tracking.
  • Cleaner step organization.
  • Saved progress on your device.
  • Quick correction and guide request workflow.

What is live now

  • Dedicated per-map pages for stronger SEO and cleaner social sharing.
  • Share preview setup added to the home page.
  • Favicon, web app manifest, and app icon setup.
  • Lazy-loaded card images and cleaner preview behavior.
  • Custom 404 page, About page, Support page, and Updates page.
  • Analytics-ready setup added for map opens and search usage.
